About OnlineObjects

OnlineObjects is a family of services developed by the Danish company Humanise.

Some of the services are public facing while others are private and requires a user account.

Who made it?

The system is currently developed and maintained by a single individual - Jonas Brinkmann Munk.

Contact Jonas at jonas@humanise.dk or call +45 28 77 63 65.

Why make it?

We had the following goals...

  • Encourage sceptical thinking
